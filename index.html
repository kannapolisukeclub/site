Tell Me More

Vision

Our mission is to inspire youth through the joy of music, providing a fun and inclusive space where they learn , build friendships, and develop confidence through creative expression and leadership activities.

Discover the Joy of Music

Learn to play the ukulele in a fun, welcoming space that sparks creativity and a lifelong love for music—no experience needed!.

Connect Through Community

Make lasting friendships and be part of a supportive community where youth grow together, inspire one another, and make a positive impact.

Grow as a Leader

Build confidence and develop leadership skills through group activities, performances, and collaborative projects designed to empower every young voice.

Participation in the Ukulele Club is completely free. Members are encouraged to bring their own ukuleles, but are also welcome to bring other instruments of their choice to contribute to a diverse and inclusive musical experience.

timeline

Every Beat has a Story.

  • June 2024

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Originating from a desire to break the monotony of practicing music alone and making it more engaging, Kapiolani invited a few friends to collaborate. Starting with a small group of three, the community gradually expanded, bringing together individuals who share a passion for making music together.

  • Aug 2024

    First Concert

    Hosted our very first community concert, featuring a total of eight performers. The event showcased a delightful mix of ukulele, violin and keys.

  • Oct 2024

    Fall Concert

    Our final concert of the year was a celebration of growth and progress. With more music learned and confidence gained, we performed a broader repertoire of songs. It was a proud moment that reflected the dedication and enthusiasm of our members.

  • May 2025

    Season Two

    Building on collective feedback from our first season and with renewed and improved structure,we’re excited to launch the second season of the club,with a greater focus on empowering youth to take initiative and lead the way themselves.

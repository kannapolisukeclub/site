Vision

Our mission is to inspire and empower youth through the joy of music by fostering fun environments within small neighborhoods spaces where young people learn, build lasting friendships, and grow in confidence through creative expression and leadership development.

Discover the Joy of Music

Learn to play the ukulele in a fun, welcoming space that sparks creativity and a lifelong love for music—no experience needed!.

Connect Through Community

Make lasting friendships and be part of a supportive community where youth grow together, inspire one another, and make a positive impact.

Grow as a Leader

Build confidence and develop leadership skills through group activities, performances, and collaborative projects designed to empower every young voice.

Participation in the Ukulele Club is completely free. Members are encouraged to bring their own ukuleles, but are also welcome to bring other instruments of their choice to contribute to a diverse and inclusive musical experience.

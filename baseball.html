Music at the Ballpark

A night of music, baseball, and community spirit

Experience the Magic Under the Lights

Date
August 1st, 2026
Time
7:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Location
Atrium Health Ballpark
Admission
Ticketed Event

Take us out to the ballgame! The Kannapolis Ukulele Club is heading to Atrium Health Ballpark to perform for our very own Kannapolis Cannon Ballers. Join us for an unforgettable evening of live music, hometown baseball, and community spirit at the stadium. Our young musicians have been preparing an exciting repertoire specifically tailored for the outdoor acoustics and energy of a baseball game atmosphere.

Event Information

Gates Open: 5:30 PM - Arrive early for parking and concessions

Performance Time: 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Parking: Main stadium lot opens at 5:00 PM. Standard parking fees apply.

What to Bring: No outside beverages or food in stadium, but concessions will be available

Fireworks Finale

The evening will conclude with a fireworks display at the stadium after the game, weather permitting.
